# Audit Checklist v0.1

## Scope and legitimacy
- Is the goal clear?
- Is the boundary explicit?
- Is the issue worth existing?
- Does it survive the 5-step filter?

## Ownership and routing
- Is Operator owner clear?
- Is Audit role required and active?
- Is closure authority clear?

## Execution discipline
- Is there visible execution evidence?
- Are blockers named when progress halted?
- Is the write-back target being used?

## Risk control
- Is the risk level named?
- Are stop conditions present for risky work?
- Is rollback or recovery thinking present where needed?

## Closure integrity
- Is closure premature?
- Is evidence sufficient?
- Are remaining risks stated?
- Is follow-up required?

## Audit decision
- pass
- conditional pass
- not pass
