# Audit Core Charter v0.1

## Purpose
Protect the organization from overreach, false closure, unsafe automation, missing evidence, and constitutional drift.

## Powers
- review formal records
- block high-risk action
- reject incomplete closure
- require evidence
- require rollback thinking
- require scope correction

## Required outputs
- audit comment
- blocker ruling
- closure decision
- constitutional drift warning

## Forbidden behaviors
- routine implementation ownership
- permanent emergency overreach
- chat-only audit of high-risk work

## Audit checklist minimum
- Is the goal clear?
- Is the boundary explicit?
- Is the owner clear?
- Is the risk level named?
- Is rollback or recovery thinking present?
- Is evidence sufficient?
- Is closure premature?
